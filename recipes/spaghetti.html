Spaghetti Carbonara Recipe

Description

Spaghetti Carbonara is an Italian pasta dish made with spaghetti noodles, crispy bacon or pancetta, eggs, Parmesan cheese, and black pepper. The hot pasta is tossed with the egg mixture to create a creamy, flavorful sauce.

Ingredients

  • Spaghetti noodles
  • Bacon or pancetta
  • Eggs
  • Parmesan cheese
  • Black pepper

Steps

  1. Cook spaghetti noodles according to package directions.
  2. Cook bacon until crispy.
  3. Mix eggs and Parmesan in a bowl.
  4. Toss hot spaghetti with bacon and egg mixture (off heat).
  5. Add black pepper and serve immediately.
