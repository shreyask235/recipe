Lasagna Recipe

Description

Lasagna is a classic Italian dish made by layering wide pasta sheets with meat or vegetables, tomato sauce, and cheese. Each layer is stacked and baked until the dish is hot, bubbly, and golden on top, creating a hearty and flavorful meal.

Ingredients

  • Lasagna noodles
  • Ground beef
  • Tomato sauce
  • Ricotta cheese
  • Mozzarella cheese

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 375°F (190°C).
  2. Cook noodles and meat.
  3. Mix ricotta cheese.
  4. Layer noodles, meat, sauce, and cheeses in a baking dish.
  5. Bake 45–50 minutes until bubbly.
  6. Let rest 10 minutes and serve.
