Pizza Recipe

Description

Pizza is a popular Italian dish made with a flat dough base topped with tomato sauce, cheese, and various toppings, then baked until the crust is golden and the cheese is melted and bubbly.

Ingredients

  • Pizza dough
  • Tomato sauce
  • Mozzarella cheese
  • Pepperoni or other toppings

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 475°F (245°C).
  2. Spread sauce on dough.
  3. Add cheese and toppings.
  4. Bake 10–15 minutes until crust is golden and cheese is melted.
  5. Slice and serve.
